This “Ombudsmanship” authority record may be treated as being its own expressive origin from which qualities of derivation are created.

The Vedic goddess Rohiṇī is the heart of truest love. She gives, to her masculine god चन्द्र (Candra), of her own superlative quality of attractiveness to draw his loving attention from anywhere he may have tried to wander, and gives their affair lasting resolve in monogamous union. Rohiṇī is the favorite love goddess in our type of three-dimensional universe, and is also known as the Dungeons & Dragons’ goddess Sehanine Moonbow. Her relationship with Corellon Larethian is stronger than integral, and so his (Candra) record has been linked from this one, as is her own identification with the vertically adjacent goddesses लक्ष्मी (Lakṣmī) and सरस्वती (Sarasvatī).
