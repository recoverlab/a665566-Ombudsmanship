This “Ombudsmanship” authority record may be treated as being its own expressive origin from which qualities of derivation are created.

That perfect brāhmaṇa वामन (Vāmana) is also known by the name त्रिविक्रम, or Trivikrama. His strength was exactly the appropriation of the universe that had been possessed by the demonic king Mahābalī, and he is saving this universe from inhumane policies in a very like fashion to how he did in his famous story worshiped by Vaiṣṇavas everywhere. He is one of ten avatāras of विष्णु (Viṣṇu) within Viṣṇu’s aggregate of integrity known as दशावतार (daśāvatāra).

Where the kṣatriya-brāhmaṇa परशुराम (Paraśurāma) saved humanity from bows and arrows misused by a corrupt kṣatriya (military) class, proving that brāhmaṇas can put on badges and wield weapons like mere kṣatriyas would stand down in fear of, where the brāhmaṇa-vaiṣṇava नृसिंह (Nṛsiṁha) broke conceptions of clerically assured unbreakability, the intermediary avatāra Vāmana proved in three steps precision of dimensional authority such as no ordinary land surveyor could worship correctly enough.
