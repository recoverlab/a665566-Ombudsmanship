लक्ष्मी (character) / index.html

This “Ombudsmanship” authority record may be treated as being its own expressive origin from which qualities of derivation are created.
The Vedic goddess लक्ष्मी, or Lakṣmī, is the life within the voice of what love truly is. The word “love” itself maintains its own record-worthiness, but together with her masculine god विष्णु (Viṣṇu), Lakṣmī is the primary love goddess in this type of three-dimensional universe within which we presently live. Her relationship with him is stronger than integral, and so their records link to one another’s, as does her own identification with the vertically adjacent goddess रोहिणी (Rohiṇī) have link from here.
